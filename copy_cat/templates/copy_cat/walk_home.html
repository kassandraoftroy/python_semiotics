WALK.A2B

finds the shortest path(s) from one word to another by linking through near neighbors on a 300-D word manifold.

    > > > > > > it repeats the process until it has landed on WORD.B

    • useful for: 1. paradigmatic analysis; 2. finding a good mixture/melange of the two words (the midpoints); 3. finding topics and word neighborhoods that connect and intersect with both words.
